## Project Overview
This Excel project analyzes AtliQ Hardware's customer and market net sales performance from FY 2019 to FY 2021. AtliQ Hardware is a leading computer hardware company that sells its products through various channels including retail stores, direct sales, and distributors. The project focuses on analyzing net sales growth trends, assessing discount effectiveness, and identifying high-performing customers and markets to optimize sales strategies and enable data-driven business decisions.

## Data Model Overview

This data model follows a star schema design with:
- **Fact Table:** `fact_sales_monthly`
- **Dimension Tables:** `dim_customer`, `dim_product`, `dim_market`, `dim_date`
- **Relationships:** Properly established one-to-many relationship for accurate aggregation and reliable reporting

## Key Insights

- In 2021, the company's total Net Sales increased by 304.5% compared to 2020, showing a strong recovery after a weak year.
- Most of the company's sales in 2021 came from Amazon, AtliQ e-Store, and AtliQ Exclusive, which are the main sources of revenue.
- Customers like Nova, Integration Stores, and Chiptec showed very high growth from 2020 to 2021, meaning their purchases increased sharply as new clients.
- From 2019 to 2021, total Net Sales grew from $87.5M to $598.9M, showing steady growth every year.
- In 2021, most of the company's sales came from India, the USA, and South Korea, which contributed the most to total sales.
- However, the company missed its 2021 target by 8.4% because of low sales in Poland, Canada, and Spain.
